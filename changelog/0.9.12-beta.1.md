## Security update

As a security precaution, we rotated the credentials Videorc uses when you
connect a YouTube account. This version ships with the refreshed credentials.

**Please update.** Once the old credentials are retired, earlier versions will
no longer be able to connect or reconnect to YouTube. Your existing recordings,
scenes, and settings are unaffected — after updating, YouTube connect and
streaming keep working as before.
